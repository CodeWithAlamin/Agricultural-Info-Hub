Grains

Grain crop is any plant that produces a small, hard, dry seed or fruit that is harvested for human or animal consumption. The term encompasses several categories of grain-producing plants, most notably cereals and legumes.

Details

Fruits

Fruit crops are plants cultivated for their edible, seed-bearing fruits, which are important for human nutrition, agriculture, and economies worldwide.

Details

Vegetables

Vegetable crops are plants cultivated for their edible parts, such as leaves, roots, stems, flowers, or fruits, which are used for human consumption to provide essential nutrients and fiber.

Details

Cash crops

Cash crops are agricultural crops that are planted for the purpose of selling on the market or for export to make profit, as distinguished from subsistence crops planted for the purpose of self-supply of the farmer.
